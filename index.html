üìö Glossary & FAQ

Simple explanations of automation terms - no jargon!

üî§ Key Terms Explained

ü§ñ API (Application Programming Interface)

Think of it as a waiter in a restaurant. You (the app) tell the waiter (API) what you want, and they bring it from the kitchen (another app). It's how apps talk to each other.

Example: Zapier uses APIs to connect Gmail with Google Sheets

‚ö° Webhook

An automatic notification. When something happens in App A, it instantly pings App B. Like a doorbell for apps!

Example: User signs up ‚Üí Webhook instantly tells your CRM

üîÑ Workflow Automation

A series of actions that happen automatically when triggered. Like dominoes - push one, they all fall in order.

Example: Signup ‚Üí Email ‚Üí Sheet ‚Üí Slack notification (all automatic!)

üéØ Trigger

The event that STARTS your automation. The "if this happens" part.

Examples: New email received, form submitted, payment made, button clicked

‚öôÔ∏è Action

What happens AFTER the trigger. The "then do this" part.

Examples: Send email, create row, post to Slack, update database

ü§ñ LLM (Large Language Model)

AI that understands and generates human-like text. Powers chatbots and smart assistants.

Examples: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini

üìä CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

Software to manage all your customer interactions, contacts, and sales in one place.

Examples: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive

üí∞ TTFV (Time to First Value)

How long until a new user gets their first "win" or sees value in your product.

Goal: Under 5 minutes for 2026 SaaS

üéØ Activation Rate

Percentage of signups who complete key onboarding steps and become active users.

Good benchmark: 60-75%+ activation rate

üìß Drip Campaign

A series of automated emails sent on a schedule. Like a drip of water - steady and consistent.

Example: Day 0 welcome ‚Üí Day 1 tips ‚Üí Day 3 case study ‚Üí Day 7 check-in

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to know how to code?

A: Nope! Tools like Zapier, Tidio, and most modern platforms are no-code. You can build powerful automations with just clicks.

Q: How much does automation cost?

A: You can start 100% FREE with Zapier (100 tasks/month), free email tools, and open-source solutions. Paid plans start around $20-30/month.

Q: What's the easiest automation to start with?

A: Welcome email automation. Sign up form ‚Üí automatic email. Takes 15 minutes, immediate value, hard to mess up!

Q: Will automation replace human support?

A: Not replace - enhance! Automation handles repetitive tasks (FAQs, welcome messages) so humans can focus on complex issues and building relationships.

Q: What if my automation breaks?

A: Most platforms send you alerts. Check task history, read error messages, and fix the issue. Usually it's simple (wrong field, expired permission).

Q: How do I know if my automation is working?

A: Always test first! Use your own email, check task histories, monitor for the first week. Most platforms have dashboards showing success/failure.

Q: Can I automate everything?

A: Not everything should be automated. Keep human touch for: complex problems, complaints, high-value customers, emotional situations.

Q: What's the ROI of onboarding automation?

A: Most companies see 60-80% time savings, 2-3x activation rates, and 40-50% reduction in support tickets within 3 months.
